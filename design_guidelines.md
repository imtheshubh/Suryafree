# SuryaFree Landing Page Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach**: Drawing inspiration from modern solar/energy companies like Tesla Solar and Sunrun, focusing on clean, professional aesthetics that convey trust and environmental consciousness.

## Core Design Elements

### A. Color Palette
**Light Mode:**
- Primary: 34 70% 50% (vibrant green representing renewable energy)
- Secondary: 47 90% 61% (warm yellow for solar/sun association)
- Background: 0 0% 98% (clean white)
- Text: 220 9% 15% (dark charcoal)

**Dark Mode:**
- Primary: 34 60% 45% (slightly muted green)
- Secondary: 47 80% 55% (softer yellow)
- Background: 220 13% 8% (deep dark)
- Text: 0 0% 95% (light gray)

### B. Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts) - modern, clean sans-serif
- **Headings**: Font weights 600-700, larger scale for hero (text-4xl to text-6xl)
- **Body**: Font weight 400-500, comfortable reading sizes (text-base to text-lg)

### C. Layout System
**Spacing Primitives**: Use Tailwind units of 4, 8, 12, and 16 for consistent rhythm
- Small spacing: p-4, m-4, gap-4
- Medium spacing: py-8, px-8, mb-12
- Large spacing: py-16, mt-16
- Container max-width: max-w-6xl with mx-auto centering

### D. Component Library
**Navigation**: Sticky header with logo left, nav links center, CTA button right
**Hero Section**: Full-viewport height with centered content and background treatment
**Service Cards**: Clean white cards with subtle shadows (shadow-lg) and rounded corners (rounded-xl)
**Buttons**: Primary green with white text, secondary outline with green border
**Forms**: Clean inputs with focus states using primary green accent

## Content Strategy & Layout

### Section Structure (5 sections maximum):
1. **Hero Section**: Bold headline "Go Solar with SuryaFree" with compelling sub-text and prominent CTA
2. **About/Mission**: Brief company introduction with sustainability focus
3. **Services**: Grid layout showcasing solar panels and water heaters with icons
4. **Gallery/Projects**: Image grid demonstrating completed installations
5. **Contact**: Contact form with company details and location

### Visual Treatments
**Gradients**: Subtle green-to-yellow gradients in hero background and section dividers
**Background**: Clean white base with occasional light green accent sections
**Contrast**: High contrast for readability, strategic use of color for CTAs and highlights

## Images
**Hero Image**: Large background image of modern home with solar panels (full viewport width, with dark overlay for text readability)
**Service Images**: Clean product shots of solar panels and water heaters (card-sized, consistent aspect ratios)
**Gallery Images**: Before/after installation photos or completed projects (grid layout, square format)
**About Image**: Team photo or facility image (medium size, integrated into about section)

## Key Design Principles
- **Trust & Professionalism**: Clean lines, consistent spacing, professional photography
- **Environmental Focus**: Green color palette, renewable energy imagery
- **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios, clear typography, intuitive navigation
- **Mobile-First**: Responsive design with touch-friendly targets and simplified mobile navigation
